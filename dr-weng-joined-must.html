Dr. Weng joined MUST

January 12, 2026

12/01/2026: Dr. Weng officially joined Macau University of Science and Technology as an Assistant Professor and independent PI. He founded his research group named ACCMaR from scratch. He will conduct research at both the LIU's Innovation and Technology Center in Macau SAR, and the Innovation Technology Research Center in Hengqin. His teaching activities will happen on the main campus of MUST.
