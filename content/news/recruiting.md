<p>We are currently recruiting researchers of the following categories.</p>
<h2 id="1-postdocs">1. Postdocs</h2>
<p>Two Postdoc positions are available in ACCMaR, with one working in Macau SAR and the other working at the Innovation and Technology Research Institute in Hengqin. The PI will offer competitive salary and benefits in accordance with university and local regulations, along with a supportive, respectful, and international working environment.&nbsp;</p>
<p class="msg msg--info"><b>Attention:</b> Postdoc candidates that are interested in working in Hengqin might be eligible for receiving extra stipend and research fund from the talent program of the municipal government. See&nbsp;<a href="https://www.hengqin.gov.cn/macao_zh_hans/zwgk/zcfg/zyzc/index.html">https://www.hengqin.gov.cn/macao_zh_hans/zwgk/zcfg/zyzc/index.html</a> for further details.</p>
<p>The following terms apply to the candidates for both positions.</p>
<h4 id="required-qualifications">Required Qualifications</h4>
<ul>
<li>Ph.D. degree in Chemistry, Physics, Materials Science, Chemical Engineering, or Computer Science.</li>
<li>Experience in theoretical/computational methods, such as density functional theory, molecular dynamics, and machine-learning methods.</li>
<li>Strong background in scientific programming using Python, Fortran, or C/C++.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to conduct independent research and publish in peer-reviewed journals.</li>
<li>Good written skills in English</li>
</ul>
<h4 id="preferred-qualifications">Preferred Qualifications</h4>
<ul>
<li>Working proficiency for communicating in English.</li>
<li>Experience in using and managing high-performance computing (HPC).</li>
<li>Familiarity with AI/ML frameworks and automatic data processing.</li>
<li>Prior work on catalysis, quantum materials, or advanced electronic-structure methods</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="2-phd-students">2. Ph.D. Students</h2>
<p class="msg msg--info">The recruitment of Ph.D. students in ACCMaR is pending until the PI acquires his eligibility for advising Ph.D. students. Please stay tuned if you are interested. Candidates are also welcome to join ACCMaR as research assistants for a 6-month program before applying.</p>
<h2 id="3-research-assistants">3. Research Assistants</h2>
<p>Multiple positions are available for research assistants. Working locations can be in Macau SAR or Hengqin. Successful candidates will be offered competitive salary and benefits in accordance with local regulations, along with a supportive and inclusive research environment.</p>
<h4 id="qualifications">Qualifications</h4>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor/Master degree in Chemistry, Physics, Materials Science, Chemical Engineering, or Computer Science.</li>
<li>Experience in theoretical/computational methods, such as density functional theory, molecular dynamics, and machine-learning methods.</li>
<li>Background in scientific programming using Python, Fortran, or C/C++.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="4-student-interns">4. Student Interns</h2>
<p>Undergraduate and Master students from MUST are all welcome to join my group as interns. You will be qualified as long as you are self-motivated by curiosity and interests. Outstanding interns will have the opportunity to publish the research work done in the group.</p>
<h2 id="5-how-to-apply">5. How to Apply</h2>
<p>Candidates are advised to specify the position they are applying for as the email title, briefly introduce yourself in the email body, and send the following documents to grweng@must.edu.mo.</p>
<p>For Postdoc candidates, please include your <b>CV</b>, and the <b>contact information for 2-3 references </b>(two documents in total) in the email.</p>
<p>For research assistants, please include your <b>CV</b>&nbsp;in the email (one document).</p>
<p>For student interns, just <b>introduce yourself</b> in the email, and no document is needed.</p>
<p class="msg msg--warning"><b>Incomplete Applications (e.g., missing materials or inappropriate email content) will not be considered. The PI will contact the candidates for interview if they pass the screening step.</b></p>
